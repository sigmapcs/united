about

Based in Southern California, the headquarters are freeway close in Cerritos, United Beverages was founded in March of 2019. The story goes much farther back and includes brand creation, launches, innovation, distribution to bars-restaurants-shops and chains, along with everything that each of those entails. United Beverages was created with a single goal: To recognize products that bring quality and value to our customers and to the end consumer, products for everyone to enjoy from fine restaurants, local bars, clubs, all levels of retail, and back to the end consumer, at home, with each one enjoying the experience.

Our brands at United Beverages are different and important to both knowledgeable and emerging wine, beer and spirits experts and consumers. Each of our products in the United Beverages portfolio, have been scrutinized, researched and specifically selected for its quality and/or its innovation along with its respect for the traditions it represents.

Each member of the team at United Beverages is an industry professional who has an extensive background in wine, beer and spirits sales, and distribution. Their knowledge of and passion for our products, combined with a commitment to customer service, have enabled United Beverages to build lasting relationships with a sophisticated, demanding, and expanding customer base.

The services United Beverages provides are second to none, yet we continue to strive to do better. Bringing the best of heritage and innovation is more than a quality seen throughout our portfolio–it is the core of our business

services

Distribution Services

United Beverages warehouse is the home for all the one of a kind items that make up our portfolio. Our wines, beers, and spirits come from all parts of the world. Our logistics team schedules daily inbound trucks and ocean containers, where all are carefully inspected upon receiving, to ensure they meet United Beverages high standards. Our warehouse staff processes orders destined for our customers throughout California and our distributers throughout the United States.

  • First Class Logistics
  • Product shipped throughout California and all of the United States
  • First Class Service
  • Brand Marketing and top account activations and placements

Importing Services

United Beverages provides complete importing services – from label approvals to container consolidation. We investigate, plan and implement effective and relevant transport methods which optimally meet the needs of United Beverages, it’s suppliers and customers.

  • Cola Label Approval
  • Cola Formula Approval
  • Import Planning
  • Compliance
  • Public Warehouse (ABC 14)
